Seminar 2023

DREAM X ENGINEERING SEMINAR 2023

Mondays 11:00 am – 12:30 pm Eastern

Each session will feature a general topic and 4 panelists who will give a brief 5-minute presentation on their key research findings related to the topic. The majority of the session will then be dedicated to an open QA and panel discussion.
Contact us for the registration link! Or view the recordings here!

    Achilleas Pavlou – Development of a Machine Learning-based nightmare detection method

    Jennifer Mundt – Lucid dreaming as a treatment for narcolepsy
    Garret Yount – Impacts of lucid dreaming on PTSD symptoms
    Nathan Whitmore – Increasing dream lucidity with a sleep-sensing app

    Emma Peters – Effects of multisensory stimulation on embodied dreaming
    Mahdad Esfahani – Dreamento: open-source dream engineering toolbox for multisensory stimulation
    Natalia Mota – Sleep onset images associated with the theta rhythm
    Guillermo Bernal – Fascia: flexible face mask for sleep recording and stimulation
